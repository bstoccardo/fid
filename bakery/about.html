About Us

Welcome to Baileys Bakery! We are a family-run business that's been making homemade goods since 1996. Our recipes have been passed down from generation to generation, starting with my grandmother famous chocolate cookies. Our bakery is dedicated to my mother's mentor and mother, Bailey Barns, hence the name Baileys Bakery.

We love to give back to the community. We host monthly karaoke nights, bingo nights, and more! Check out our events page for more information
